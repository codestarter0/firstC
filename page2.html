About of IIBM Patna

Embark on a Journey of Excellence in the Best College for MBA, BBA, and BCA in Patna.

Established in 1979, IIBM Patna stands as a beacon of educational excellence, with a legacy of 45 years in shaping the future leaders of Management, Computer Science, and IT.

As an autonomous institute recognized by AICTE, Ministry of Education, New Delhi for MBA, and Aryabhatta Knowledge University for BBA and BCA, IIBM Patna is committed to providing unparalleled education, training, and research opportunities.
